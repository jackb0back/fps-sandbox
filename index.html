OASIS FPS DEMO

A Movement fps game by jackboback





Controls

MAIN CONTROLS

Middle click to change hotbar loadout
W,A,S,D to move
Mouse to look around
0-9 to change guns
LeftShift to slide/crouch
~ to spawn a single enemy
- to spawn muiltible enemies
L for the spawn in menu (0 to use)
ESC for main menu
T to change loadout
V to noclip (with cheats enabled)
Q for jetpack
E for anti-grav grenade
Click to shoot

VEHICLES

H to enter
J to exit

BUILDING

switch to hotbar 2
Left click to break
Right click to place
Scroll wheel or number keys to change blocks

SANDBOX TOOLS

script gun => attach script with Right click and B to toggle script menu
Grab tool => Right click to select and left click to place
Delete tool => Right click to delte objects (NO UNDO)

Guns
1. Pistol
2. Rifle
3. Shotgun
4. grenade launcher
5. for EMP
6. for Super Weopon
0. for spawn in gun
